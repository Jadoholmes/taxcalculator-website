1. Deal with collections accounts
‍ Paying off a collections account removes the threat that you will be sued over the debt, and you may be able to persuade the collection agency to stop reporting the debt once you pay it. You can also remove collections accounts from your credit reports if they aren 't accurate or are too old to be listed.

2. Pay credit card balances strategically
