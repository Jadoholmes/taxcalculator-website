How is a check taxed

In America, a check, no matter the pay period, is taxed based on a set of parameters, one of which changes annually. The categories these deductions are broken up into include state tax which could differ from state and even from county to county and federal tax. State tax is a mix of medicare deferred compensation and the leftover amount to make up the percentage needed to be taken out to meet your tax bracket.
