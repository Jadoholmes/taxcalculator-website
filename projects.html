What is FICA

FICA or the Federal Insurance Contributions Act is an act that was passed in 1935 and was established in order to fund social security to give people in need (those with disabilities or other issues) financial assistance. Ever since its establishment employees are taxed with FICA taxes to help fund social security and medicare.

Why was FICA established

It was first established to help fix and prevent anything like the great depression from ever happening. Since at the time of the great depression there was no financial aid from the government social security was later put into place to give struggling people or those with disabilities financial support, FICA was passed to fund social security.
